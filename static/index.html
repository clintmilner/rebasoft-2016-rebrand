Visibility & Control

Rebasoft provides in-depth Visibility and Control into what connects to the Network and what it’s doing. We see all devices, which applications they use, who is using them in real time, process and store this information for future audit.

Importantly, we uniquely show all these complex interrelationships without the need of hardware probes or software agents and present it for technical and non-technical audiences.

This enables the three fundamental questions to be answered:

  1. What is on your network?
  2. Are you in control?
  3. Are you spending resources effectively?

Rebasoft Solutions

Application Auditor

Processes NetFlow data, as well as infromation from firewalls, switches, load balancers & proxies. Connect to Auditor Core for full forensic data.

Auditor Core

Using SNMP it can actively or passively poll every network-attached end point to identify every user, device known, or unknown to the Network Administrators.

Threat Auditor

Designed to function with your existing environment using minimal resources, Threat Auditor easily and cost-effectively deploys without agents or probes to detect known, and unknown devices on your network.

RMC

Rebasoft Management Console (RMC) is the next generation of network monitoring, detailing, reporting, and managing tools from Rebasoft. RMC provides a central managment platform to control all instances of Application Auditor and Auditor Core from one screen.
