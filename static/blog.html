Auditor Core

Forensic Knowledge of Every Endpoint

Auditor Core is Rebasoft’s intelligent discovery technology. Using SNMP it can actively or passively poll every network-attached end point, including dumb devices such as EPOS, to identify device, user and location information within complex, multi-vendor IT environments.

Auditor Core identifies previously ‘invisible’ devices ensuring infrastructure gaps are closed, secured and bought back under your control.

Centrally deployed, without the need for agents or probes, Auditor Core offers comprehensive network visibility for a relatively low capital outlay. It provides a real-time and historical view of your network to fulfill reporting, compliance and inventory management requirements.

Scenarios

External Challenge: Breaches of the new European PCI DSS regulations could result in fines of up to 3% of an organisation’s global turnover. Auditor Core can demonstrate compliance with key requirement such as 7. Restrict Access to cardholder data and 10. Track and monitor all access to network resources.

Internal Challenge: Auditor Core accesses the information already held within your infrastructure and identifies several unknown devices at remote locations presenting unmanaged security risks. By finding these devices, Auditor Core brings them back into your secure and managed environment.
